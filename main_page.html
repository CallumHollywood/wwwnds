ABOUT NEXUS DATA SCIENCE

End-To-End Data Science Solutions for Business and Science

Nexus Data Science is the brainchild of Callum Hollywood, a multi-disciplinary data scientist with a rich background in both business and environmental sciences. We provide a wide range of data science and machine learning consultancy services that leverage insight from data to guide business and science decision makers.


We are open-source technology enthusiasts that…

  • Rise to the challenge to deliver cutting-edge solutions for complex data-driven problems
  • Explore and reveal novel business opportunities from data derived from a variety of industries
  • Provide end-to-end open source (license-free) solution installation and maintenance
  • Consider all local (South Africa) and international (everywhere else) contracts

Callum Hollywood, Senior Data Scientist BSc., MSc.




Our services are delivered extensively with R and Python – the top two open-source statistical and data visualization programming languages used across the globe. We specialize in integrating R and Python with SQL databases, BI platforms, and Linux servers, to offer comprehensive end-to-end data science solutions.

We will work with your in-house teams to best understand your unique data situation and tailor solutions to meet your requirements.

We enjoy unlocking insights from your data with robust statistical analyses and data visualizations that help guide you to make the most informed decisions.
